About Driverwanda

Driverwanda is a Rwanda-based transportation solution launched in 2023, dedicated to providing reliable, affordable, and convenient mobility options. Starting as a small car rental service, we have grown into a comprehensive platform that connects customers, drivers, and car owners, enhancing travel experiences while supporting the local economy. Our vision is to revolutionize transportation in Rwanda by leveraging technology to meet diverse needs, from daily commutes to special events.

Our Goal

The primary goal of Driverwanda is to bridge the gap between car owners, drivers, and customers, offering a seamless platform for transportation services. We aim to:

  • Make car rentals and driver hiring accessible, affordable, and efficient across Rwanda.
  • Create job opportunities for drivers and revenue streams for car owners with unused vehicles.
  • Enhance mobility and ensure safe, reliable travel for all users.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be a one-stop online solution for transportation needs in Rwanda. We are committed to:

  • Providing high-quality, well-maintained cars for rentals, with options for self-drive or with a driver for weddings, events, tours, and daily use.
  • Building a trusted marketplace where drivers can find jobs and customers can request skilled, verified drivers.
  • Facilitating airport pickups and custom transportation services tailored to individual preferences.
  • Allowing car owners to rent out unused vehicles, earning passive income while promoting sustainable resource use.
  • Ensuring privacy, security, and transparency, with all data handled confidentially by our team.
  • Promoting road safety through verified driver applications (including license checks, experience, and language skills) and regular vehicle maintenance.

We cater to weddings, birthdays, conferences, tours, and daily commutes, managing all interactions securely. Contact us for personalized offers!
